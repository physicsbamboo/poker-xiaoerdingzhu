# 趣味历史知识 / Fun Historical Facts

## 关于"三反"和"五反"的历史背景

### 中文版

在「小二定主」这款扑克牌游戏中，"三反"和"五反"作为特殊的主牌规则，其名称来源于中国历史上的重要政治运动。

**三反运动（1951年）**

"三反"运动是1951年12月至1952年10月期间，由毛泽东等中国共产党高层领导发起的政治运动。全称为"反对贪污、反对浪费、反对官僚主义"（简称"三反"）。

**五反运动（1952年）**

"五反"运动是1952年1月至10月期间开展的针对私营工商业者的政治运动。全称为"反对行贿、反对偷税漏税、反对盗骗国家财产、反对偷工减料、反对盗窃国家经济情报"（简称"五反"）。

**游戏中的体现**

在「小二定主」游戏中：
- **三反**: 当启用时，三张3成为主牌
- **五反**: 当启用时，三张5（除♦5外）成为主牌，且强度高于三反

这些规则名称巧妙地借用了历史术语，使得游戏规则更加生动有趣。在游戏中，五反的强度高于三反，这也与历史时间顺序相符（五反运动在1952年，晚于1951年的三反运动）。

### English Version

## Historical Background of "三反" (Three Anti) and "五反" (Five Anti)

In the card game "小二定主" (Xiao Er Ding Zhu), the special trump card rules "三反" (Three Anti) and "五反" (Five Anti) derive their names from significant political campaigns in Chinese history.

**Three Anti Campaign (1951)**

The "三反" (Three Anti) Campaign was a political movement launched by Mao Zedong and other high-ranking Chinese Communist Party leaders from December 1951 to October 1952. The full name was "反对贪污、反对浪费、反对官僚主义" (Oppose Corruption, Oppose Waste, Oppose Bureaucratism), abbreviated as "三反".

**Five Anti Campaign (1952)**

The "五反" (Five Anti) Campaign was a political movement targeting private industrialists and merchants from January to October 1952. The full name was "反对行贿、反对偷税漏税、反对盗骗国家财产、反对偷工减料、反对盗窃国家经济情报" (Oppose Bribery, Oppose Tax Evasion, Oppose Theft of State Property, Oppose Shoddy Workmanship, Oppose Theft of State Economic Intelligence), abbreviated as "五反".

**In the Game**

In "小二定主":
- **三反 (Three Anti)**: When enabled, all three cards of rank 3 become trump cards
- **五反 (Five Anti)**: When enabled, all three cards of rank 5 (except ♦5) become trump cards, and they are stronger than 三反

These rule names cleverly borrow from historical terminology, making the game rules more vivid and interesting. In the game, 五反 is stronger than 三反, which also aligns with the historical timeline (the Five Anti Campaign in 1952 came after the Three Anti Campaign in 1951).
